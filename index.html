About Me

Hi! My name is Shobowale Idowu AdeniyiI love creativity, communication and inspiring challenges. I'm 23 years old and a graduate of library and information science from Tai Solarin University of Education, presently a Masters student of University of Ibadan, Nigeria. Studying Library, Archival and Information Studies. I'm deeply passionate about design, especially in terms of branding, identifying and packaging designs. I want to work with people that have the same passion and vision as i do. Basically, i am a designer with deep passion for design and who is looking to create something extraordinary.

I'm obsessed with making things and even more obsessed with making things better. My specialty is front-end web design and UI/UX designs
